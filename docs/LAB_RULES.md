\# Eurolab Pro Laboratory Rules



\## ICP Formula



Final Result (g/L) = Instrument Reading (mg/L) × Dilution Factor ÷ 1000



\## Titration



KCN-Au = Volume × 0.13

H2SO4-Rh = Volume × 2.45

Ag Content = Volume × 1.079

KCN-Ag = Volume × 6.5

Pave Bath Cu = Volume × 3.1785



\## Dilution Factor



DF = Original Standard Concentration ÷ Instrument Reading of Standard



DF remains valid only for the current calibration run.



\## Units



ICP Output = mg/L

Report Output = g/L



\## Recommendation



Recommendation depends on:

\- Bath Capacity

\- Optimal Range

\- Current Result
